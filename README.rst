Abstract
========

This article addresses the issue of evaluating the quality of recommender systems in the long term, taking into account the evolution of consumers and product assortments. We consider the dynamic system of changes in consumers and products over time. The aim of the study is to determine the conditions under which audience degeneration, assortment degeneration, or transaction distribution degeneration occur in this multiple machine learning system, as well as to develop an enhanced trainable recommendation algorithm capable of improving metric performance without degeneration of distributions. Additionally, the results of experiments with various machine learning algorithms to find the optimal recommendation algorithm that enhances standard metrics without degeneration in the long term perspective are described.
